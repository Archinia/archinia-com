What is it to be Called?  
  
Like the A on her chest  
worn for all to see  
but with none of the guilt  
and all of the fear  
  
At least her beloved  
touched her private parts...  
laid her bare...  
ravaged her soul...  
  
Mine...  
may well eat mine.
