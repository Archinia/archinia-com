Not Just for entrepreneurs: How to REALLY make it once you're out in the real world.

  

_"You spent a lot of time getting an education. _

_But if you want to make it as an entrepreneur, it's time to forget some of what you learned:_

  

_1\. If you only do what you're told, you'll excel._

_2\. Being micro-managed is to be expected._

_3\. Your time off is the highlight of the year._

_4\. Getting criticized means you failed._

_5\. Success is based on toeing the line."_

  

**_None of these are true, if you REALLY want to make it._**
