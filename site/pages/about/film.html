Rachel started making films in 2012 when she was asked to produce a video class for the local university. She trained at Upublic.tv in Albuquerque, as well as with NatGeoTV at sites throughout New Mexico. She also studied acting with David Sinkus and Christopher Lloyd, and worked on a major motion picture before producing her own work.
