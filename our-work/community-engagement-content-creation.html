Community Engagement +
Content Creation


An influencer herself, Rachel works with historic sites, cultural centers, tourism organizations, architects and preservationists, and media outlets to produce relevant, engaging multi-media content that emphasizes the personal experiences that set a place apart.

Rachel harnesses her passion for the Genius loci - Spirit of Place - to help our clients improve engagement and awareness, fundraising potential, social engagement (likes and shares), and dollars spent at your venue!

Rachel has explored architectural and cultural perspectives from Okinawa, Japan to Santa Fe, New Mexico to Vicenza, Italy.
